IOS DEVELOPMENT




WHY WE'LL LEARN IOS DEVELOPMENT?


iOS development is the process of making mobile applications for Apple hardware, including iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch. iOS is based on the Macintosh OS X and supports the programming languages Swift, C, C++, and Objective-C.

BENIFITS OF IOS DEVELOPMENT


1. Designing and building advanced applications for the ios platform.

2. The iOS environment is more integrated.

3. Troubleshoot and fix bugs in old and new applications.

4 . iOS updates are more predictable.

5. iOS developer resources are streamlined and readily available
